# Grandma Wisdom Content

## Purpose
Creates emotionally resonant, culturally grounded wisdom-based content.

## Prompt
Create 5 “grandma wisdom” style quotes based on the topic: [insert topic].

Tone:
- Warm
- Direct
- Wise
- Slightly humorous

## Example

Input:
Topic: Knowing your worth

Output:
“Baby… anything that has to be convinced don’t deserve you.”
