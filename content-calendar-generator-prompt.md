# Content Calendar Generator Prompt

## Purpose
Creates a structured multi-week content calendar for a women’s empowerment brand using AI.

## Prompt
Create a [number]-week content calendar for a women’s empowerment brand focused on [theme].

Each week should include:
- Main topic
- Spiritual insight
- Tarot card connection
- “Grandma wisdom” message
- 3–5 TikTok/Reels ideas
- 1 YouTube video topic
- 1 engagement question
- 1 monetization CTA

## Example

Input:
Create a 4-week content calendar focused on feminine energy and self-worth.

Output:
Week 1: Stop Proving Your Worth  
- Tarot: Empress  
- Insight: Your presence is the prize  
- Reels: “Stop auditioning for love”  
- YouTube: “Why Women Over-Explain Themselves”  
- CTA: Download self-worth guide
