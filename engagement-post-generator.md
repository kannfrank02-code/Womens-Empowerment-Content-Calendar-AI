# Engagement Post Generator

## Purpose
Creates Facebook or community posts that drive interaction and conversation.

## Prompt
Create an engaging social media post based on [topic].

Include:
- Relatable statement
- Emotional hook
- Question for audience

## Example

Input:
Topic: Emotional burnout in relationships

Output:
“How many times have you poured into someone who barely poured into you?

Be honest… when did you start feeling drained?

Drop your answer below.”
