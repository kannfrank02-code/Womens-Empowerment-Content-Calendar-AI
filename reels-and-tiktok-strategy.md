# Reels and TikTok Strategy

## Purpose
Generates high-engagement short-form video ideas for TikTok and Instagram Reels.

## Prompt
Create 10 short-form video ideas for TikTok/Reels based on the topic: [insert topic].

Include:
- Hook
- Key message
- Emotional angle
- Call to action

## Example

Input:
Topic: Stop chasing men

Output:
1. Hook: “The moment you stop chasing is when everything changes”
   Message: Attraction over pursuit
   CTA: “Comment ‘detached’ if you’re done chasing”
