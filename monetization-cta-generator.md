# Monetization CTA Generator

## Purpose
Creates calls-to-action that convert content into sales or leads.

## Prompt
Create 5 call-to-action statements for [product or service].

Include:
- Emotional trigger
- Clear action
- Benefit

## Example

Input:
Product: Soft Girl Commandments book

Output:
“Ready to stop chasing and start attracting? Grab your copy today.”
