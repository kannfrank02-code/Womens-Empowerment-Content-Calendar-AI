# Tarot Content Integration

## Purpose
Blends tarot meanings into real-life empowerment and relationship advice content.

## Prompt
Explain how the tarot card [card name] relates to [topic] in a way that is practical, emotional, and easy to understand.

Include:
- Card meaning
- Real-life application
- Feminine energy insight

## Example

Input:
Card: Queen of Cups  
Topic: Emotional boundaries

Output:
The Queen of Cups represents emotional depth, but also emotional protection. She teaches women to care deeply without overextending themselves.
